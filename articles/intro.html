Personal Digital Assistant

Introduction

Artificial intelligence (AI) is increasingly being used in human daily activities. Today it is becoming a part of our life. Every smartphones’ operating system comes with a personal AI assistant to make our lifestyle easy. Searching, Planning, Messaging, Scheduling many more cognitive tasks can be done with the help of PID.Google's Assistant, Apple's Siri, Microsoft's Cortana, Amazon's Alexa are obvious examples of personal digital assistants. Sometimes you may not even know what the customer service agent that you were speaking with is a virtual assistant. These assistants continuously gather information and learn many things about us. Over time personal digital assistants gather enormous amounts of information. This document introduces the concepts of ethics, discusses the ethical issues of virtual assistants and discusses technology and learning techniques for personal digital assistants.

What Are Digital Assistants?

A personal digital assistant or virtual assistant (also known as a predictive chatbot) is voice-activated software that can understand and carry out electronic tasks. Without using your hand to pull up any certain app or program you can do your task.Artificial intelligence(AI),natural language processing,machine learning,natural language understanding are used digital assistants to learn and to provide a conversational experience to user.It combines historical information such as location, purchase preferences,so on,data models can be added by algorithms to identify patterns of behavior and after that refined patterns are added as data.Personal digital assistant can answer questions , give recommendations,have conversation by learning user’s history , preferences and many other gathered information.
